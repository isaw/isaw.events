==============
plone template
==============

Use paster::

    >>> paster('create -t plone plone.example --no-interactive')
    paster create -t plone plone.example --no-interactive
    ...

Let's check the content::

    >>> package_dir = os.path.join('plone.example', 
    ...                            'plone', 'ploneexample')
    >>> ls(package_dir)
    __init__.py
    configure.zcml
    locales
    tests.py
